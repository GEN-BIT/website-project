    SOD — Dept. 01

    Software Development

    Programming, web development and custom software — building Rwanda’s digital economy.

    Curriculum & Skills

    • Web DevelopmentHTML5, CSS3, JavaScript and responsive website design.
    • Database ManagementMySQL, data modelling and backend administration.
    • Programming FundamentalsPython, PHP and software engineering principles.
    • Computer NetworkingIP configuration, LAN setup and network administration.
    • Real Client ProjectsSchool portals, inventory systems and business apps.
    • IT Support & HardwareTroubleshooting, OS installation and technical support.

    ETE — Dept. 02

    Electrical Technology

    Industrial and domestic electrical systems, solar energy and power infrastructure across Rwanda.

    Curriculum & Skills

    • Domestic InstallationHouse wiring, circuit breakers, lighting and safety compliance.
    • Industrial WiringThree-phase systems, motor controls and factory infrastructure.
    • Solar & Renewable EnergySolar PV installation, charge controllers and inverters.
    • Electrical Safety StandardsInternational safety codes, testing and hazard prevention.
    • PLC & Automation BasicsIntroduction to programmable logic controllers.
    • Blueprint ReadingElectrical drawing interpretation and schematic documentation.

    ELE — Dept. 03

    Electronics

    Repair, maintenance and design of electronic systems — a high-demand skill across Rwanda.

    Curriculum & Skills

    • Circuit Design & AnalysisAnalogue and digital circuits, PCB design, component ID.
    • Consumer Electronics RepairTV, radio, audio equipment and mobile phone servicing.
    • Industrial Control SystemsSensors, actuators and electronic control panels.
    • Microcontrollers & ArduinoEmbedded systems and automation project builds.
    • Testing EquipmentOscilloscopes, multimeters and signal generators.
    • Renewable Energy ElectronicsSolar charge controllers, inverters and off-grid systems.

    BDC — Dept. 04

    Building Construction

    Training skilled masons, site supervisors and construction consultants for Rwanda’s growing sector.

    Curriculum & Skills

    • Masonry & BricklayingFoundation laying, wall construction, plastering and tiling.
    • Structural Design BasicsArchitectural drawings, calculations and load analysis.
    • Site ManagementConstruction planning, materials management and on-site safety.
    • Concrete TechnologyMix design, formwork, reinforced concrete and quality testing.
    • Roofing SystemsTimber roofing, iron sheet installation and waterproofing.
    • Plumbing BasicsDrainage systems, water supply and sanitation infrastructure.
