    How to Join

    Admission Requirements

    To Apply for Any Program You Must:

    • Hold a Rwanda National Exam certificate (Primary Leaving Exam or O-Level)
    • Meet the Rwanda TVET Board minimum entry score requirements
    • Submit a completed application form at the school admissions office
    • Provide two recent passport-size photographs
    • Provide a copy of your National ID card
    📅 Intake Periods: February and September every year
