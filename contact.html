    Experience

    What Saint Laurent Gaseke Is Really Like

    Nestled in the scenic Gicumbi District of Northern Rwanda, Saint Laurent Gaseke Technical Secondary School is more than just a training facility—it's a community dedicated to transforming young people into skilled professionals.

    ⚙️
    Modern Technical Facilities

    We provide state-of-the-art laboratories and workshops where students work with real industry-grade equipment and tools used in professional settings.

    👥
    Expert Instructors

    Our faculty members are experienced professionals with real-world expertise in their fields, bringing practical knowledge directly into the classroom.

    🎓
    Industry-Focused Programs

    Every program is designed with input from industry partners, ensuring that graduates have the exact skills employers are seeking.

    🌱
    Supportive Learning Environment

    We foster a collaborative atmosphere where students support each other while developing leadership, teamwork, and problem-solving skills.

    Our graduates go on to succeed in software development companies, electrical engineering firms, construction companies, and electronics manufacturers across Rwanda and beyond. We're proud of our track record of producing professionals who make a real impact in their fields.

    How to Join

    Admission Requirements

    To Apply for Any Program You Must:

    • Hold a Rwanda National Exam certificate (Primary Leaving Exam or O-Level)
    • Meet the Rwanda TVET Board minimum entry score requirements
    • Submit a completed application form at the school admissions office
    • Provide two recent passport-size photographs
    • Provide a copy of your National ID card
    📅 Intake Periods: February and September every year
